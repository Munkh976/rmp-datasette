About EPA Risk Management Plans

The Environmental Data & Governance Initiative (EDGI) provides this tool to help citizens explore data from the EPA's Risk Management Plan (RMP) program. This program requires facilities that use extremely hazardous substances to develop plans to manage risks and prevent accidents.

Our interactive map and database allow you to explore facilities near you, view accident history, and understand the potential risks in your community. The data is updated regularly to provide the most current information available.

Data Exploration

Search for facilities by location, industry type, or chemical. Filter results to find exactly what you're looking for and export data for your own analysis.

Visualize Trends

View charts and graphs that illustrate accident frequency, common chemicals involved, and industry patterns to better understand environmental risks.

Community Resources

Access guides, fact sheets, and educational materials to help communities understand and address environmental risks in their area.
